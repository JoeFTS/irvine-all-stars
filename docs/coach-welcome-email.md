**Subject: Welcome to Irvine All-Stars -- Coach Portal Access & Getting Started**

---

Hi Coach,

Congratulations on being selected as an All-Star coach for the 2026 season! We're excited to have you on board.

We've built a dedicated site to manage the entire All-Star process -- from tryouts and scoring to rosters, compliance, and tournaments. Everything you need is in one place at:

**https://irvineallstars.com**

---

### Getting Started

1. **Check for Your Invite Email** -- You'll receive a separate email invite from the site with a link to set up your account. Use that link to get started.

2. **Create Your Account** -- Follow the link in the invite email to register. You'll be asked to fill out some basic info about your coaching background.

3. **Log In** -- Once your account is set up, log in at https://irvineallstars.com/auth/login and you'll be taken directly to your Coach Dashboard.

---

### What You Can Do on the Site

**Tryouts & Scoring**
- View all registered players in your division
- Download a pre-populated score sheet with your division's players
- Score players on a 1-9 scale across 6 categories (Hitting, Fielding, Throwing, Speed, Effort, Attitude)
- Upload your completed score sheet
- Select and submit your player recommendations

**Team Management**
- View your roster with player details and parent contact info
- Track which players have signed contracts
- Monitor compliance status for each player (birth certificates, photos, medical releases)

**Certifications (Required)**
- Upload your Concussion Protocol and Sudden Cardiac Arrest Prevention certifications (required by California law AB-379)
- Track assistant coach certifications
- Free training links are provided on the site

**Tournaments & Rules**
- View upcoming district tournaments, schedules, and registration deadlines
- Access the 2026 PONY Baseball Rulebook
- Reference pitch count rules for your division

**Compliance Dashboard**
- Your dashboard shows your overall compliance progress
- Action items tell you exactly what's still needed
- Key dates and deadlines are displayed on your timeline

---

### First Steps After Logging In

1. Review your dashboard and familiarize yourself with the layout
2. Check the **Help** page if you have questions about any feature
3. Start working on your **certifications** -- these take time and are required before tournaments
