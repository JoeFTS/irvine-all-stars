### 2. Supabase email rate-limit fix
Parents activating invites were getting "email rate limit exceeded" because Supabase's builtin SMTP caps at 2 emails/hour and `mailer_autoconfirm` was off.

Wired Resend as Supabase custom SMTP:
- `smtp_host: smtp.resend.com`
- `smtp_port: 465`
- `smtp_user: resend`
- `smtp_pass: <RESEND_API_KEY>`
- `smtp_admin_email: AllStars@irvineallstars.com`
- `smtp_sender_name: Irvine All-Stars`
- `rate_limit_email_sent: 1000` (per hour)
- `mailer_autoconfirm: true` (signup skips confirmation email entirely)

13 affected parents got reply drafts in Gmail. Saved memory at `project_supabase_email_rate_limit.md`.
