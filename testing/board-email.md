# Email to Board — Copy & Paste

**Subject:** Help Us Test the New All-Stars Website Before We Go Live

---

Hi everyone,

I'm excited to share that the new Irvine All-Stars website (irvineallstars.com) is ready for beta testing. This platform will handle everything for our All-Stars program — player registration, tryout management, team selection, parent notifications, coach binder compliance, and tournament readiness tracking.

Before we go live with families and coaches, I'd like the board's help testing the site to catch any issues. Your feedback will make a big difference.

**What I Need:**

- **2-3 board members to test as a Parent** — Walk through the parent experience: register a test player, accept a selection, sign a contract, upload documents, and complete a medical release.

- **2-3 board members to test as a Coach** — Walk through the coach experience: manage a team binder, upload certifications, review the roster, and check pitching logs.

- **1-2 board members to test as an Admin** (President/VP preferred) — Walk through the admin experience: review coach applications, create teams, manage tryouts, and track compliance.

**How to Get Started:**

The site is invite-only, so I'll need to create your account. Here's what to do:

1. **Reply to this email** with the email address you'd like to use and which role you want to test (Parent, Coach, or Admin)
2. I'll send you an invite link to set up your account
3. Once you're in, follow the attached Testing Guide (PDF) for step-by-step instructions on what to test

**A few things to know:**

- **You don't need real documents.** When the site asks you to upload a birth certificate, photo, medical form, coaching cert, etc. — any random image from your phone or computer is fine. We're just testing that the upload functionality works, not verifying actual documents.
- **Same goes for forms.** You can fill in fake names, addresses, and info. We're testing that the forms work correctly, not collecting real data.
- **There are already test players preloaded** in the system with sample information, so you'll have data to work with right away — you don't have to start from scratch.

**Please test on both your phone and your computer.** The site should work great on both, and I want to make sure it does.

**Timeline:** I'd like to have testing done by the end of next week so we can address any issues before tryouts open.

The attached Testing Guide has detailed instructions for each role, including exactly what to click, what to look for, and how to report any issues you find.

Thank you for helping make this season's All-Stars experience the best one yet.

Joe Hernandez
All-Stars Coordinator
AllStars@irvinepony.com
